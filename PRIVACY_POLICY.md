# Privacy Policy - TimeManager

**Effective Date:** January 25, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to TimeManager ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and login
- **Name**: Optional, for personalization
- **Profile Picture**: Optional, uploaded by user
- **Password**: Encrypted and securely stored

### 2. Usage Data
- **Tasks and Events**: Your to-do items, calendar events, and notes
- **Focus Sessions**: Pomodoro timer usage and productivity data
- **AI Chat History**: Conversations with our AI assistant
- **App Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (screens visited, features used)

### 3. Device Information
- **Device Type**: Phone model and manufacturer
- **Operating System**: Android version
- **App Version**: TimeManager version you're using
- **Device ID**: Anonymous identifier for push notifications

### 4. Automatically Collected Data
- **IP Address**: For security and fraud prevention
- **Log Data**: Error logs and crash reports
- **Cookies**: For web app authentication

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Provide Services**: Enable core app functionality
2. **Account Management**: Create and maintain your account
3. **Sync Data**: Synchronize across devices
4. **Notifications**: Send reminders and alerts
5. **AI Features**: Power the AI chatbot assistant
6. **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries

### Secondary Uses
1. **Improve Services**: Analyze usage to enhance features
2. **Bug Fixes**: Identify and resolve technical issues
3. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
4. **Communications**: Send important updates (opt-out available)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- **Location**: Secure cloud servers (AWS/Google Cloud)
- **Encryption**: 
  - In transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  - At rest: AES-256 encryption
- **Backups**: Daily automated backups
- **Retention**: Data kept while account is active

### Security Measures
- JWT authentication with refresh tokens
- Password hashing (bcrypt)
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Regular security audits
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
